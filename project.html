ABOUT
PROJECT.

Project Jaladhi aims at educating and creating an awareness among the people whose life are solely dependent on water bodies. Thus, creating an impact on Habitat Loss, Overfishing, Climate Change and Pollution.

The aim of the project is basically to come up with sustainable solutions to the problem of ocean pollution that can be incorporated in our daily lives. To achieve our goal, we will be hosting several webinars, workshops and social media campaigns to create awareness.

The first webinar was held on 21st March 2021 with Supritha Vijaykumar, a sustainability professional and entrepreneur with the sole purpose to make the youth aware of the eminent threats related to ocean pollution and the solutions for the same. We aim to continue to do this even in the future to reach to a wider audience.
